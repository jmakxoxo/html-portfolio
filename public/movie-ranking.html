The Best Series According to Jen

My Top 3 for 2023

The Patient

A great thriller analyzing a sociopath but also about self reflection

Fleishman is in Trouble

A mini series about real life. It touches on topics that are somewhat taboo to speak about: midlife crisis, childhood traumas and how they effect your exeryday life, the problems of society, expectations of women, mental issues.

The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel

A hilarious take on how life was in the 1950s and 60s.
